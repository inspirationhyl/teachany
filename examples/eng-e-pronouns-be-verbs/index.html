❓

疑问句变形

I am → Am I?
He is → Is he?
They are → Are they?
把be动词移到句首！

🚫

否定句变形

I am not = I'm not
He is not = He isn't
They are not = They aren't
在be动词后加 not！

💬

常用对话

A: What is your name?
B: My name is Tom.
A: How old are you?
B: I am eight.
