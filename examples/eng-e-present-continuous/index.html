✨ 现在进行时公式
主语 + am/is/are + 动词-ing
例：I am reading. / She is running. / They are playing.
☀️
🧒

📌 am/is/are 怎么选？
am
只用于 I
I am eating.
is
he/she/it/单数名词
She is sleeping.
are
you/we/they/复数
They are playing.

🎮 第二关
🔮 第二关：动词变形魔法
📚 -ing 变形三大规则
规则① 直接加-ing（一般动词）
read→reading · eat→eating · play→playing · sleep→sleeping
规则② 去掉e，再加-ing（以不发音的e结尾）
make→making · write→writing · dance→dancing · come→coming
规则③ 双写末尾字母，再加-ing（CVC结构）
run→running · swim→swimming · sit→sitting · get→getting
🎯 拖拽连线：找到正确的-ing形式
